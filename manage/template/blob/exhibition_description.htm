Additional Protocols for the event

*** any protocaol like the COVID protocaol below *** The recent increase in number of COVID cases due to the Omicron strain, could force the Government to impose restrictions on public gatherings and conduct of events. The information published above is subject to change should the Government of Karnataka decide to impose such restrictions. These may result in restricted partcipation or even cancellation of the event in the last minute. We request participants to refer to this page for the latest status on the Exhibition. Even otherwise we are required to follow standard COVID protocols prescribed by the Government. Here are a list of protocols that the visitors and participants must adhere to:

  • Persons attending should follow the Covid Appropriate Behavior prescribed by the Govt. of Karnataka. This includes social distancing, wearing of mask and vaccination.
  • All visitors must pass the Thermal screening test done by YPS at the entrance.
  • Entry to the award function is restricted to members of YPS and participants of [contest-name] and their families.
  • During the Award Function priority will be given to recipient of awards.
  • We may have to ask visitors to wait to limit the number of people at the venue within permitted numbers. We strongly advise visitors to choose lean hours for visiting the exhibition.
  • Every visitor will also be asked to sign a Covid declaration before being admitted to the exhibition.
