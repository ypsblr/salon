Suman Saha, a businessman by profession and a photographer by heart is deeply in love with his camera in search of people and places for the last 26 years. Achieving distinctions, awards and acceptances happen to be just milestones in a long journey with his photographs. Visiting various exhibitions, attending photo seminars and witnessing various images while at jury's chair is a vital part of his journey as a photographer. Through his love to learn and love to coordinate by associating with different photographers, he enlightens himself gradually.
