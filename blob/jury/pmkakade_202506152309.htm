FIP MEMBER ID : LM013667

Ms. Prema Kakade is an artist by nature having dabbled professionally in various forms of art like Batik, Tanjore and Mysore paintings. These pieces of art have reached innumerable countries through the prestigious Karnataka State Handicrafts Development Corporation. 

During her childhood she was exposed to photography by Mr. E Hanumantha Rao, the renowned wildlife photographer. Joining YPS in 2016 catapulted her passion for photography and brought her UP Lalit Kala Academy Award, Karnataka Tourism Award as well as many awards from national and international salons. Her works have won Acceptances from over 40 countries. She has been a jury member in many national, international salons and competitions. Her pictures and articles have been published in several digital and print media. 

 She says "Photography to me is not only an extension of my art form, but goes much deeper as a meditative media."

Her interest in photography includes various genres like pictorial, nature, wildlife, macro and travel. She has served as Chairperson for YPS Salons. She is presently serving as the Secretary of Youth Photographic Society, Bengaluru.
