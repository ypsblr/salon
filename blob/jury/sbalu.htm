S. Balu is a Civil Engineer and Builder by profession and a photo hobbyist. He is into photography for the past 3 decades. He started his photography during film days and now continuing in the digital era. His area of interest was pictorial photography during film days. Later he got attracted towards nature and wildlife photography.

He is a Life Member of Photographic Society of Madras (PSM), Federation of Indian Photography (FIP) and a member of Photographic Society of America (PSA).
