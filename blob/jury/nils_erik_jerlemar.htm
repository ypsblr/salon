Nils-Erik Jerlemar is the Initiator and Co-founder of the Nordic Society of Photography (Nordiska Förbundet för Fotografi - NFFF) and its President since it started in 2015. He was elected as the President of the National Association of Swedish Photography (Riksförbundet Svensk Fotografi - RSF) in 2013.

Nils-Erik Jerlemar started photography in 1969. He presently lives in Malmö, in the southest part of Sweden. He became a member of the Photographic Society of Malmö in 1972. After a break, due to professional pressures, he started competing again in 2004.

He has chaired over 40 International Digital and Print Exhibitions including over 10 from Photographic Society of Malmö. His photographs have won innumerable number of awards including YPS International Salon.
