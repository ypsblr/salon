Mr. Bhupesh Chandra Little, is the Dean of Student's Welfare and In-charge of Photography at College of Arts and Crafts, Lucknow from which he did is Master of Fine Arts in Photography. He is the Chairman of Horizon International through which he had organized 5 International Exhibitions

He has won 35 International Awards from 10 different countries and 150 National Awards. Over 1500 of his works were accepted in International and All India exhibitions. His works are on display at the ‘Metropolitan Museum’ Spain, and ‘International Federation of Photography’ France. One of his images was also printed in the Platinum Jubilee Coffee Table Book of PSA. He is a co-author of the book 'Experimental Photography'.
