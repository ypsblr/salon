Saurabh Bhattacharya is associated with the Research & Technology department of a renowned paint company and has a deep passion for playing with colors.

His journey in photography began in 2004 with his admission to the Photographic Association of Dum Dum, Kolkata. He achieved his first Salon acceptance in 2008 and has since garnered over 3,000 international acceptances from 87 countries. He holds photographic distinctions from all eight international bodies, a testament to his dedication and skill.

Photography has provided him with a stress-free life and continually inspires him to think outside the box.
