Arjun Haarith hails from the garden city of India, Bangalore, and works as a Senior Software Engineer on Adobe's Lightroom team.

His passion for photography was kindled at an early age and honed through regular outings with H. Satish, a renowned wildlife photographer and former President of YPS (Youth Photographic Society). These experiences helped him master the art of photography. Arjun began exploring nature photography during his college years (2007-2008) and has since diversified into other genres, including travel, pictorial, astrophotography, and more.

An avid traveler, Arjun loves discovering new places and documenting his journeys through beautifully crafted blogs. His blog site http://arjunhaarith.blogspot.in boasts over 40 entries, offering vivid descriptions of destinations, practical tips on accommodations, must-visit spots, and stunning travel photography. A passionate trekker, Arjun has also scaled numerous peaks in the Himalayas and the Western Ghats, capturing the breathtaking beauty of these regions.

In the world of competitive photography, Arjun has over 1,500 acceptances from national and international salons, and has garnered more than 250 awards at both levels. His work has also been recognized with Honorable Mentions in prestigious photography contests such as Maria Luisa (Spain) and DJMPC.

Arjun's photographs have been featured in esteemed publications like Sanctuary Asia and showcased in calendars, books, and digital platforms, including Nat Geo India, BBC Earth, and Nat Geo Traveller.
