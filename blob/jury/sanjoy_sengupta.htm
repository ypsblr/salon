Sanjoy Sengupta is presently the Director of Mentor Services at Photographic Society of America. He is an Indian photographer presently living in Muscat, Oman. He is the second Indian to achieve GMPSA back in 2019 and is also the second Indian to achieve GMPSA/Bronze in 2021.

An engineer by profession, photography is a self-taught skill for Sanjoy Sengupta. Combined with his passion for travel, photography provided the right platform to capture people, cuture, architecture and tradition from places in Europe and South East Asia that he visited.

He has over 7000 acceptances from International Salons and 300 awards from 92 countries.
