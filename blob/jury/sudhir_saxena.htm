FIP Member ID : LM 389


Mr Sudhir Saxena has been pursuing photography for more than 45 years and has travelled extensively for photography. He has won many medals and trophies from FIAP and PSA and has a few thousand photographs exhibited across the world. He has also served as a Jury member in many International Salons since 1984.

Mr Sudhir has published photobooks on Ghats of Varanasi and Kumbh. His one-man exhibition "Sadhus" was well appreciated by photolovers.

He is the founder of Photolovers Photographic Society and has organized 16 International Photography Circuits so far, including 4 Grand Circuits. Presently, he is a member of the Working Committee of the Federation of Indian Photography.
