Ms. Barbara Schmidt graduated in Adminstrative Sciences and worked for 40 years as a civil servant. She hails from Ruhr area, Germany. She has been on the road with her Fuji XT2 since 2014.

Ms. Barbara is a self-taught photographer. Her interests in photography are varied. She loves photographing everything that arouses her interest. She does not visit places with some specific pictures in mind and comes back with pictures of common subjects presented in her own unique point of view. This approach has helped her beat the stereo-type and come up with unique and successful images. Shapes, lines and colors presented in everyday life interests her.

Genres of interest for Ms. Barbara Schmidt are, Architecture, Landscape, People, Nature, Travel, Street and Animals. She has won many awards and recognitions since 2015.
