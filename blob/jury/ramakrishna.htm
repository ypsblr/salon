Ramakrishnaiah is a keen field biologist and nature photographer and has traveled extensively in India and abroad. More than 500 of his images have been exhibited in over 25 countries and have won over 50 awards including 20 Gold medals.


Currently he is Dean academic at Government Science College, Bengaluru besides heading the Department of PG Studies and Research in Biotechnology. With over 25 years of teaching and research to his credit, he has published 25 papers in peer reviewed journals and has guided Ph D students.

He is trained in Indian Navy and presently Commissioned as a Naval officer in the Rank of Lieutenant with NCC responsibilities. He was conferred with "Best Officer Cadet" and honored with DG NCC Commendation for outstanding service.
