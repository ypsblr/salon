Bhaskar Athavale, an Instrumentation Engineer by profession, initially received photography lessons from Mr. Sharad Devare & Vishwas Morye. What began as a hobby gradually transformed into a passionate pursuit. Bhaskar enjoys capturing images across various genres of photography, with a particular affinity for nature (birds & wildlife), landscapes, and travel scenes.

Throughout his journey, Bhaskar has garnered several acknowledgments, including international distinctions such as MPSA, EFIAP/Silver, GPU Aphrodite - Crown 3, CMOL, GAPU, Hon CPE, Hon WPAI, FFIP, and APSI. He has accumulated approximately 3500 international acceptances in 53 countries, accompanied by 175 awards.

Notably, Bhaskar has received the BEST LANDSCAPE Trophy for the years 2019, 2021, and 2023 from the Photography Society of India (PSI). Furthermore, he has contributed to the photography community by publishing an article on "Photographing Birds in Flight" in the monthly magazine of PSI - CLICK (Aug 2022) and in the WPAI Newsletter Jan 2023.

Bhaskar's expertise has been recognized in the international photography circuit, as evidenced by his nominations as a Jury member in more than 25 International Salons.
