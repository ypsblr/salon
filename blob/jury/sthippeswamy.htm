A 77 year old veteran in Photography and Cinematography, he has over 50 years of experience in both. During his long years of service to photography, he has served as professional cinematographer, photojournalist, teacher, administrator and mentor in several organizations. The documentary films shot by him have won many national awards. 

Recipient of Karnataka Media Academy Award, he served in Karnataka Lalith Kala Academy and many Govt. Organizations that promote photography. He was also awarded the prestigious Karnataka State Rajyothsava Award in 2015. He is a proud receient of Vayosreshta Sanman National Award from the President of India for outstanding contributions to creative Art and Wildlife Photography.

Over a span of 30 years more than 25000 pictures of Mr. Thippeswamy have been published in News Papers, magazines and journals. His pictures were selected 13 times in World Cup Wildlife Photography Competitions.

He has been on jury panel of countless numer of Exhibitions, Award Selectiond and Contests.

He served as an Executive Committee Member of FIP for 9 years. He continues his journey as a teacher and mentor and organizes Wildlife Photography workshops.
