Mr. C R Sathyanarayana, is a Top Exhibitor in the world in Nature Photography as acknowledged by Photographic Society of Amaerica's Who is Who. He has won a whopping 10,000 plus Acceptances in Pictorial and Nature photography. This includes over 1000 Awards and Certificates in International and All India Salons.

Mr. Sathyanarayana was a member of the team that represented India in the FIAP NATURE BIENNIALS WORLD CUP events held in 2001, 2003, 2008, 2012, 2016 and 2018 which won many AWARDS for INDIA. He was one of the Key Persons in organizing the FIAP NATURE BIENNIAL WORLD CUP in INDIA in the years 2010 and 2014 held at Bangalore. He was a Working Committee member of FIP during 2013-2014.

Born in 1956, he holds degrees in Commerce and Law. He is a Managing Partner in Rajaram Textiles, dealing in Silk Sarees in wholesale and retail space. He is a former Tennis Player and represented Karnataka twice in National Championships.
