Mr. Dhritiman Mukherjee is very well known for his work on rare, endangered and challenging subjects. His photographs have been frequently published with brands like BBC, National Geographic, New York Times, Lonely Planet, London Geographic, WWF, UNESCO and Birdlife International. He is a widely-travelled wildlife photographer covering most of the national parks in India and abroad. He has won many national and international awards and accolades, the Carl Zeiss Conservation Award 2013 being one of them. He is one of the founders of the “Saevus” wildlife magazine in India.
