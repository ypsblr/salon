Dr. Christine Cope Pence, QPSA, is an International Business Developer and an Educator professionally. She is a wildlife photographer by passion. Pandemic period aside, she travels widely from her current base in California. She is the PSA Pictorial Image Division (PID) Chairperson for 2020-2022. For PID, she runs Interclub competitions and this year will be responsible for the new Open Monochrome Interclub competition. Since joining PSA in 2014, she has been involved in many international exhibitions as both a participant and as a judge.
