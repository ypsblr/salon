Ms. Ana Joveva, has a Masters in Science and is an Engineer, Architect and Designer. She has managed many projects in constructing public buildings and hospitals for over 20 years. The last 10 years, she was working as consultamt for hospital building projects in Middle East and Europe. She is presently working with Ministry of Health, Macedonia overseeing construction and re-construction of hospitals.

She loves playing Piano in addition to her love for photography. In a professional world of dealing with people and rules, she finds, in Photography, an avenue to express herself. The genres in which she specializes are, Abstract, Still Life and Street Photography and love compiling Travel stories as blogs.

Ms. Ana Joveva is the PSA Exhibition Standards Director for Region 13, North Macedonia. She is a recipient of many awards and nominations for her photographic work.
