Born in Rome in 1954, Umberto D'Eramo now resides in Sulmona and is self-employed. He serves as an Adjunct Professor in the School of Medicine's Department of Neurology at the University La Sapienza, with affiliations in both Rome and Palermo. His professional expertise lies in the field of scientific research, where he specializes in digitalized biometry and movement analysis. Additionally, he has pursued amateur photography as a hobby since 1974. Over the years, he has actively participated in numerous photography competitions, achieving notable success and exhibiting his work in Italy and abroad. A significant accomplishment in his photography journey includes winning the Digital Photography category at the X Edition of the 2015 Florence Biennale of Contemporary Art.
