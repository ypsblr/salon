Madhu Sarkar established the National Academy of Photography (NAP) in Kolkata in 2003 to provide a platform for aspiring professional photographers to acquire required skills through intense and innovative methods. He continues to be the Director of NAP and has guided hundreds of aspirants to achieve excellence in various genres of photography.

MADHU SARKAR was still in his tender teens when he identified photography as the medium of his artistic expressions. He stood by his passion and vision throughout his life against all odds. His photographic works are characterized by a unique blend of gloom and grandeur and reflected reality of life with intense emotions.

Madhu Sarkar has won many international honors from all over the world.
