Mr Vaibhav S. Jaguste served as the Vice President of The Photographic Society of India, Mumbai which is one of Asia's oldest photography institutions, established in 1937.

A regular participant in the National and International Photography salons, Mr Vaibhav has won over 100 awards & 1000 acceptances from 23 countries. His work is highly appreciated by the Federation of Indian Photography and has honored him with the prestigious Fellowship (FFIP) in 2011. He was awarded with EFIAP distinction by Federation International d'La Art (FIAP) in 2014. He has also judged numerous national and International Salons wholeheartedly.

His exhibitions "Hymns of Nature" and "Rocks and waves" at the prestigious Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai was greatly supported by the Print media and art industry. He also led his Club along with national executives to Seoul, South Korea and Morocco and Marrakech to attend the FIAP Congress in 2016 and 2019 respectively.
