Ms. Hema Narayanan is a professional Photographer, Writer & Photography Mentor [Engineer by Education, Artist by choice] and a Graduate in Photography from the New York Institute of Photography. After working in the IT field for more than a decade, she quit to become an entrepreneur and founder of the firm 'Wider Angles' in the photography and writing industry. Her work is frequently published in travel magazines, journals, and newspapers. As a licensed artist with Getty Images and Shutterstock, she leads 'Experience India' photography journeys under her banner. She undertakes 'Commercial photography' projects in urban spaces, home decor & interiors, people and family portraiture, lifestyle, architecture, and products. 

She is currently working as a Consultant Photojournalist in a non-profit organization working on Chronicling Covid-19 in India.
