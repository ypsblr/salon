Paul James is from Wales in the United Kingdom and is a member of 'Port Talbot Camera Club' and 'Bridgend and District Camera Club' where he is the Programme Secretary. Paul is also a member of the executive committees of the Welsh Photographic Federation and the Photographic Alliance of Great Britain.

Paul enjoys immersing himself with photography and is always happy to support other photographic clubs and institutions in any way. Paul’s favourite genre of photography is Portrait Photography and he has a studio permanently set up at home and regularly has friends and family visit for a portrait session. The majority of Paul’s portraits are headshots.

“It’s all about eyes and getting my model to show me emotion. Happiness, sadness, craziness, aloofness are some of the emotions that I seek to capture”, says Paul.
