GURDAS DUA, FIIPC, FBAF, AFIP, Hon. FPASP, Hon. GMSAP, Hon. ADCC stands as an internationally acclaimed photographer who has graced the world of photography with his creative brilliance since 1989. His unwavering commitment to education, a long list of accolades, honors and an impressive portfolio of published works are testimony of invaluable contributions to the realm of photography which has garnered him recognition and praise from numerous renowned global, local and government organizations. His remarkable skills and contributions have garnered widespread acclaim, earning him distinguished honors and memberships in numerous photography organizations.

Beyond his affiliation with the Press Club of Indore, Gurdas Dua holds the prestigious role of Founder-Director at LENS MAESTRO GROUP (https://lensmaestrogroup.com/home). The members of this group work for mutual benefit of amateur and seasoned Photographers across the world, who wishes to hone their skills in the field of Art of Photography and get a global recognition for their work. Gurdas has the honors of serving as a jury member for various international competitions, including:

HIPA, Dubai (Hamdan Bin Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Makhtoum International Photography Awards), Raw Photography Lab, Italy organized Emotions and Urban Life Photo Contests, Dodho Magazine, Barcelona, Spain Photography contests in years 2017, 18, 19, 20, 22, 23 & 24. Photographic Society of America organized Individual Portrait Contest 2023, FIAP organized World Cup for Clubs 2023, Oasis Photo Contest 2024 at Italy. Photographic Society of America organized Individual Still Life Contest 2024. Additionally, he has judged many National and International Salons and Photo Contests

Five times National Award Winner in National Contests (Organized by Ministry of Information Broadcasting & Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Govt. of India). He was chosen to be among the Top 30 exhibitors of the world by National Geographic for the year 2008. Awarded for “ADHA ASMAN HAMARA” Photo Contest on Women Empowerment organized by The Department of Women and Child Development in 2001 

Epson Color Imaging Award at Japan in 2004. Gujrat State Lalit Kala Akademi award in 2005. He was a member of delegation of Indian photographers to Pakistan for Art for Peace mission in March 2006. Over 1000 Photographs Exhibited and over 100 Awarded in Indian and International Exhibitions.
