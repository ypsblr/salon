Murali Santhanam is a keen Nature and Landscape Photographer. He has been associated with Youth Photographic Society over the last 10 years. During this period he has built a grat collection of macros, birds, landscapes and city-scapes. He is quick to the adoption of technology in his photography work and uses many techniques like HDR, Exposure Blending, Focus Stacking etc. to get the best out of his subjects.
