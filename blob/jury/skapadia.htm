Mr. Sunil Kapadia has been a photographer since 1980 with an inclination towards Fine Art Photography. During the journey he has won more than 400 National and International Awards for his works. He has been behind many exhibitions and workshops. He has also been part of the Jury for many Salons.

Mr. Kapadia had been the President of R. A. Acharya Memorial Photographic Club, Mumbai, India and The Photographic Society of Pune, India. He has written several articles on photography in some leading local photography magazines. A number of his pictures have been published and are being sold as art form. He also teaches photography.

Mr. Kapadia's prime force of photography is 'Light' and he tries to see light first then the subjects.
