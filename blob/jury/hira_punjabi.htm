Mr Hira Punjabi has been doing wildlife photography for the last 26 years and won many awards nationally and Internationally. His photography works have been published in more than 25 books and hundreds of magazines. His solo photography exhibition on wildlife during the wildlife week in October 2000 at Bombay Natural History Society and a group show in Tangent Art Gallery in October 2003 was well received in the photography community.

He was adjudged "Photographer of the year" by photographic Society of India in the year 2000, and also won first prize in "Sanctuary ABN wildlife photographer of the year" photography competition in the year 2001 and second prize in the year 2004. He is also the only Indian photographer to have won Glanzlitcher Photo Competition Awards, Germany over 3 times.
