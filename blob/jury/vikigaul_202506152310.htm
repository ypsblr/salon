Viki spent her adult life as a potter/ceramist/painter. About 24 years ago she was gifted the Nikon FE2 and many rolls of film by her father, Fred Greene, Hon PSA, GMPSA. He said when she learned how to use it, he would give her a digital camera and mentor her in the art of post-processing using Photoshop. She joined the Photographic Guild of Nova Scotia, and soon after, the Photographic Society of America and the Canadian Association for Photographic Art. ‘Competition Photography’ became her passion, and 20 years later, it continues to fulfill her need to be creative.

Viki has held several roles at her club, and in the Photographic Society of America. She has received many awards through the years but is most proud of the ten times her name appears on her club Photographer of the Year trophy, and her most recent

achievement, the Gold level of the PSA Portfolio Distinction Program.

Viki will tell you, “It is important to immerse yourself in a photography club or organization, and … ‘putting in’ what you expect to ‘get back’, is the perfect combination for success.”

She believes that photography is the best hobby in the world!
