Luis Alberto Franke has been a member of the Board of Directors of The International Federation of Photographic Art (FIAP) since 2017. He is also serving as the Director of FIAP Biennial Service and as the FIAP Liaison Officer for Argentina. He is an active member of the Grupo Fotografico Parque, an important photo club of Buenos Aires. He also is an honorary member of Photo Club Elema from Skopje (Macedonia)

He was borne in Argentina and is an Industrial Engineer by profession. His photography shows the natural sceneries, the people and especially they show the rural life of the vast southern farms. His photographic reports of Southern Patagonia, Argentina have won him wide acclaim. He was selected as the "Best of the Best Author" by FIAP in 2012 and 2013.

He has over 15000 acceptances from International Salons and 2000 awards from 60 countries.
