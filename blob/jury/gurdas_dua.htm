Gurdas Dua, FIIPC, FBAF, AFIP, Hon. FPASP, Hon. GMSAP, Hon. ADCC, is an internationally celebrated photographer whose creative journey began in 1989. With a strong dedication to education, a wealth of accolades, and a significant portfolio of published works, he has earned recognition from esteemed global, local, and government organizations. As the Founder-Director of LENS MAESTRO GROUP, he fosters skill development and global recognition for photographers worldwide.

Gurdas has served as a jury member for prestigious competitions, including HIPA (Dubai), Dodho Magazine (Spain), FIAP World Cup, and the Photographic Society of America contests, among others. He is a five-time National Award winner (Ministry of Information Broadcasting & Ministry of Tribal Affairs, India) and was listed among the Top 30 global exhibitors by National Geographic in 2008. His notable awards include the Epson Color Imaging Award (Japan, 2004) and the Gujarat State Lalit Kala Akademi Award (2005).

His work, with over 1,000 exhibitions and 100 awards, has been featured in global publications like Global Photographic Union Magazine (Greece) and Smart Photography. Notable articles include “Get-Set-Go for Travel Photography” (2015) and “How to Modify a Portrait to Photogenic” (2019).
