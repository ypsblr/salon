Ms. Neetu Katyal is a passionate photoartist who has achieved remarkable success in the field of photography and has established herself as a prominent figure in the realm of photoart with close to 1000 acceptances from 21 countries. She joined TPAS (Tricity Photo Art Society), Chandigarh in 2015 and currently, she dons the mantle of President. She also runs a Fine Art Portraits Studio.

Neetu Katyal has also been honored with an Award of Excellence by First Friday Forum, Chandigarh (Oct'18) and an Award of Excellence by "WE" in collaboration with Punjab Arts Council (March'17).

As the only female photoartist in Tricity to have been honored with such prestigious distinctions, Neetu Katyal is motivated to continue learning and sharing her knowledge of photoart. She enjoys working on various genres such as portraits, travel, conceptual, and street photography. Her passion for learning and spreading knowledge in the field of photoart drives her to excel and make a lasting impact.
