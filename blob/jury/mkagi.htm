FIP MEMBER ID LM013324

Being an Automotive Engineer Mr. Mahalingaraju Kagi worked 20+ years in the Sultanate of Oman. Photography was his passion since early school days. He is an amateur photographer interested in art, travel, nature, street, sports, culture and fashion photography. He is a trained Fashion Photographer from J. D. Institute of Fashion Technology Bangalore.

.He is a member of Federation of Indian Photography (FIP) & Photographic Society of America (PSA). He is also a member of PSA Exhibition Data Review Team since Jan 2020. His works have been exhibited across eighteen countries and received awards and honours. He is the Secretary of the Photographic Club of Bangalore.
