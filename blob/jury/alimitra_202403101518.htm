Piyali Mitra, a graduate in Microbiology and holder of a Masters in Biotechnology, has harbored a deep love for nature since her childhood days. Growing up in close proximity to a renowned national sanctuary fueled her interest in wildlife. Her passion for nature blossomed into a full-fledged addiction after completing a photography course at Chhayapath, Calcutta.

She has traversed extensively across India, capturing vibrant festivals, events, and slices of tribal culture and tradition. Piyali has garnered significant recognition for her work, including the prestigious distinction of GMPSA/Bronze from the Photographic Society of America in 2022 and Crown 4 from the Global Photographic Union. She further solidified her reputation with accolades such as EFIAP/Diamond 1 from FIAP in 2023, as well as EFIP and EFIP Gold (Nature) from the Federation of Indian Photography.

As a Life Member of the Federation of Indian Photography since 2013, Piyali has honed her skills through various courses, including Light Painting, Photography in Available Light, and Advance PTD from the Photographic Society of America. Her dedication has been rewarded with over Eight Thousand Acceptances and 600 awards from different National and International Salons.

Her captivating portfolio has graced exhibitions numerous times, notably at Lalitkala, Uttar Pradesh, where she has received recognition. Additionally, Piyali has been honored with an award from Lalitkala, Gujarat. Beyond government contests, she has participated in numerous national-level competitions, consistently earning awards and exhibitions.

Piyali's primary areas of interest lie in Human Interest, Travel, and Wildlife Photography, reflecting her deep connection with the natural world and diverse cultures.
