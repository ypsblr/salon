Living in Belgium (EU), Jacky had always been interested in photography, but other interests kept him from pursuing it seriously until his retirement. After retiring, he began taking photography lessons, joined a local photo club, and became a member of FIAP (Fédération Internationale de l'Art Photographique) and PSA (Photographic Society of America) to participate in national and international photo competitions.

What began as a hobby gradually evolved into a deep passion. His photographic interests span Nature (especially Macro), Landscapes, Cityscapes, Architecture, Sports, Streetlife, and Travel photography—with a particular affinity for India. In Streetlife and Travel photography, he focuses on capturing people engaged in their everyday activities.
