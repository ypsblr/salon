Satish is the President of Youth Photographic Society Bangalore. He is a Photographer by Profession with a keen interest in all genres of photography. He has won over 800 awards in various National and International Salons. He has been bestowed with Lalith Kala Academy Award from New Delhi and Lucknow. He has judged over 50 National and International Salons. His works have featured in many magazines and calendars.
