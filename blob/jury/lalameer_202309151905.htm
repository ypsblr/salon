Amal Alameer is an award-winning photographer and an international photography Jury member based in Makkah, Saudi Arabia. As of 2023, she has obtained more than 1500+ international awards. She is the founder and president of the New View Photography Club. She is also the chairman of the New View contest and the Gulf Circuit. Translator in the Arabic version of PSA Magazine. Member of many photography organizations around the world. She has organized eight international photography competitions.
