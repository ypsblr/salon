Manasi Deb Sarkar, a resident of Jalpaiguri, a small town in the northern Indian state of West Bengal, identifies herself as a serious amateur photographer and photo-journalist, having embarked on her photographic journey in 2017. Throughout her pursuit, she has achieved notable milestones such as obtaining the EFIP distinction in 2021 and the EFIAP/b distinction in 2023.

Manasi's talent has garnered recognition at several national-level exhibits and has earned her numerous awards. Additionally, she has been honored with the role of a jury member at national-level photographic salons on multiple occasions.

Her passion lies in capturing the essence of people and places, with a particular interest in environmental portraits. Manasi attributes much of her success and growth to her association with JPA since 2018, which has provided her with a robust platform and unwavering support. She is also a proud member of both FIP and FIAP since 2019.

Currently, Manasi serves as a correspondent photo-journalist with Uttar Banga Sangbad, where she continues to showcase her talent and dedication to the field of photography.
