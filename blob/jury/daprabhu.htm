Mr. Dinesh Allamaprabhu comes from a heritage of natures lovers and photographers. After a brief stint in IT Industry, Mr. Allamaprabhu decided to return to his family business of professional printing services and focus on Photography. His association with YPS helped him to start participating in Salons. He has won over 75 awards from 43 countries with over 950 exhibits.

He is a passionate nature-lover. His interest in photography is primarily driven by his fondness for animals and observing their behaviour. He loves spending quality time in the tranquility of nature and capturing the finer moments. He draws inspiration from his father, who is a renowned nature photographer from the film era. He took up photography to share his experiences through the lens.

Mr. Allamaprabhu along with his father continues to promote film photography.
