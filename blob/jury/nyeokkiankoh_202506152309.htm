Jenn Yeokkian Koh is a Malaysian photographer and businesswoman whose passion for photography began in earnest in 2015, when she completed a three-month course in digital and basic photography organized by the Photo-Art Society Batu Pahat (PSBP).

Since then, she has actively contributed to the photographic community, currently serving as Vice Secretary of the Photographic Society of Kluang (PSK), and as a committee member of both the Photo-Art Society Batu Pahat (PSBP) and the Photographic Society of Malaysia (PSM).

Encouraged by her mentor, Jenn began participating in international photography salons in 2017. That same year, she earned the Proficiency (PPSA) distinction from the Photographic Society of America (PSA). Her achievements continued with the Excellence (EPSA) and Master (MPSA) distinctions in 2018, followed by the Grand Master PSA (GMPSA), EFIAP, and GPU VIP 5 distinctions between 2021 and 2023.

To date, Jenn has received over 500 awards and more than 6,000 acceptances in international photography competitions. She has served as a judge for national photography competitions since 2020 and has been invited to jury international exhibitions since 2021. She currently holds the role of Star Ratings Director (PTD) for the Photographic Society of America (PSA).

Jenn’s photographic journey began with a fascination for macro photography—particularly insects—and has since evolved to include a wide range of genres including landscape, minimalism, and travel photography. Her work is driven by a desire to tell visual stories and capture moments that evoke emotion and curiosity. For Jenn, photography is not only a form of creative expression but also a meaningful way to find joy and release from the stresses of daily life.
