FIP Member LM-030072

Mr. Anil Risal Singh is the former President of Federation of Indian Photography and is the President of Lucknow Camera Club. He was the former Head of Photo Division, National Research Laboratory for Conservation of Cultural Property, Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India, where he was bestowed with Senior Fellowship.

He was born at Agra, India in 1954, graduated from Lucknow Christian College in 1974, completed post-graduation from Lucknow University in 1976. He then earned a Diploma in Photography from Institute of Science & Technology, Mumbai.

Over the last 45 years, he had won around 250 National & International Awards and had held 20 solo exhibitions in India and 2 in Atlanta, USA. His pictures were also part of the Group Exhibition at Place de Festival, Canes, France. He was awarded Charles Wallace Professional Grant to visit and study photographic collection in different museums in UK.

He had been a Jury member of several National & International Salons of Photography. He has also been teaching photography in many Institutes & Organizations and acted as a guest lecturer / examiner / paper setter for various Indian Universities.
