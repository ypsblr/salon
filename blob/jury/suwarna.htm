Dr. Adv. Suwarna Gawde is a highly decorated personality in not just academics but also in the field of photography.  She has been involved with The Photographic Society of India, serving as a member of the managing committee from 1994 to 1998, Hon. Secretary from 2015 to 2017, and then again from 2019. She was Salon Secretary for the 18th International PSI Salon in 2019 and has judged several international photography competitions by invitation.

As a photography faculty member, she teaches Basic and Advanced Photography and Photoshop. She has won numerous awards in various national and international Salons of Photography, with over 1000+ acceptances. Her solo exhibition on the theme "Kokan" was held at Jehangir Art Gallery, Mumbai in 2014. 

Her extra-curricular activities include painting, bonsai, reading, and wildlife and bird photography. She has traveled extensively for wildlife and photography.
