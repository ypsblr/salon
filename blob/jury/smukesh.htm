A qualified Engineer with Post Graduation in Digital Electronics from Indian Institute of Science, Mr. Mukesh Srivastava made a successful foray into digital photography. He resides in Dhanbad, Jharkhand. 

To explore the world through his lens, he has travelled to Australia, UK, Holland, Brussels, France, Switzerland, Germany, Italy, Austria, South Korea and Iceland. 

Few of his images are being sold in the USA as Greeting cards. His works have been published in Lonely Planet, Syndic Literary Journal, Better Photography, Smart Photography, Asian Photography, NatGeo Magazine and Outlook Business. 

He has more than 150 National and International awards to his credit. To name a few, he received 27 from Kodak, 4 from NatGeo, 1 from Savewater (Melbourne) and 75 awards in International Salons recognised by the FIAP and the PSA. 

He has trained more than 8000 photographers through workshops at Center For Visual Arts, Nikon and IPA, Kolkata. He was Nikon’s Official Mentor from 2015 to 2017. Presently he is a visiting faculty at Institute of Photo Arts, Kolkata and Dept. of Mass Communication, BMKU, Dhanbad
