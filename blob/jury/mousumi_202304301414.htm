Mousumi Phukan is a housewife from Guwahati, Assam who began her photography journey in 2016. She specializes in wildlife photography but also enjoys shooting in various genres. In 2019, she achieved her first photographic distinction, AFIP, from the Federation of Indian Photography. In 2020, she received her second distinction, EFIP, and her third distinction, AFIAP, from the Federation International de l'Art Photographique. Mousumi is also a member of the Federation of Indian Photography.
