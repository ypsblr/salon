Meet Rana Jabeen, a dentist by profession but a photographer at heart, hailing from Mumbai, India, and currently residing in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Over the span of the last 20 years, Rana has embarked on numerous journeys to fascinating destinations around the globe. Each locale holds its own allure, enriched with history, culture, and unique architecture, aspects that Rana delights in exploring and experiencing firsthand.

Rana's preferred genres of photography include cityscapes, landscapes, architecture, and long exposure shots. An aficionado of architectural imagery, Rana finds inspiration in the intricate patterns, lines, forms, geometry, and textures found within structures. Beyond mere passion, photography serves as a means for Rana to connect with oneself. Amidst the technical aspects, photography has imparted valuable life lessons, such as the virtues of patience, meticulous planning, adaptability to sudden changes, and most significantly, the art of slowing down, breathing in the present moment, and reveling in the beauty of art in its myriad forms.
