T A Jayakumar is a keen nature and wildlife photographer. He is a specialist in Macro Photography, specializing in Butterflies, wild flowers and insects. It was a proud moment when he won Honorable Mention in the prestigious BBC Wildlife Photography Contest in 1999. He has won several awards and certificates in nattional and international salons. He was part of the jury in several YPS Salons and contests and hence is no stranger to participants of YPS Salons. He is an Information Technology specialist by profession.
