Mr. Gurdas Dua believes in teaching what he has learned. He has conducted innumerable workshops covering varied professional topics. He owns a fully equipped studio in Indore. His areas of specialization in photography includes Pictorial, Nature, Wildlife, Travel, Portrait, Architectural, Advertising, Industrial & Fashion. He is a member of Indore Press Club and a life member of many photographic bodies like IIPC and FIP.

Some of his awards include five times National Award in contests organized by Ministry of Information & Broadcasting organized by he Govt. of India. He is the author or several articles on Photography in reputed Indian magazines like Smart Photography and Better Photography and also in International Magazines like National Wildlife Magazine, USA. His photographs have also been published in News Papers and Magazines on innumerable occasions.

Mr. Dua is a Brand Ambassador for Sony Alpha cameras.
