Mr. Pinaki Ranjan Talukder has been practicing photography for over 40 years. He joined as member of Chayapath, Kolkata in 1983 and since then there is no looking back. He won his first acceptance in the same year and since then he has won more than 500 awards and acceptances in national and iternational salons.

He has been trained as a Salon jury by Photographic Association of Dum Dum in 1999. Since then he has been a judge on several national and international salons for the past 20 years bringing in wide perspective with professional approach.

Mr. Talukder also held several positions on the committee of Chayapath.
