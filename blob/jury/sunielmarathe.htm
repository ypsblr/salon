Suniel is a globally recognized photographic artist and educator with over two decades of experience. He has served as a jury member for 125+ international exhibitions and chaired 20+, showcasing his deep commitment to photographic art and ethics.

He has conducted 60+ workshops across India, focusing on Portraits, Fashion, Glamour, and Fine Art Nude photography, alongside leading distinction programs and image evaluations for major clubs like PSI and FFPC.

Suniel holds several prestigious honors, including:

GMPSA (2020) – youngest Indian recipient

ARPS (RPS, UK) and EFIAP (FIAP) – both in 2013

GAIUP (IUP, China, 2019)

Dual FPSI Fellowships – a unique distinction in PSI history

FFIP from the Federation of Indian Photography

With 1,300+ international awards from 65+ countries and 15,000+ exhibited images, his work has global reach. He has also held key roles in PSA, including Director, ERD, and ROPA Assistant Director.

Suniel continues to inspire through his mentorship, education, and global engagement in photography.
