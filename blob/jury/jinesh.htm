Mr. Jinesh Prasad hails from Moodabidri in Mangalore. His journey as a photographer started during the negative film era. He joined India International Photography Club in 1985-86 and participated in several workshops by legends like Late Sri T.N.A Perumal, Sri N.Sunder Raj, Sri Allama Prabhu H N, Sri O.P Sharma, Sri B.W Jatkar and many others.

Travelling has been Mr. Jinesh’s passion since childhood.  He has covered many temple car festivals, events like Kambala, Bhuta Kola and other cultural activities. He was an official photographer for mega events like the Mahamastakabhisheka at Shravanabelagola in 1993 and many other Mastakabhishekas at  Karkala, Venur, Dharmasthala and other places.

He is a budget solo traveller and has visited many places in India and countries like Malaysia, Cambodia, Thailand, Laos, Indonasia, Vietnam, Srilanka, Dubai, AbuDhabi, Bahrain and Bangladesh.

He joined YPS in 2018 and started to participate in Salons and Circuits. In the short span of two years, he has won more than 150 international and national awards and more than 2000 exhibition acceptances.
