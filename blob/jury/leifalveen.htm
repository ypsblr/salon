Mr. Leif Alveen is a Danish (Denmark) photographer with a lifelong interest in taking pictures. After a lengthy break from photography, he got back into it around 10 years ago, and since 2010 he has been a regular participant in international photo competitions, resulting in more than 400 awards from around the globe and has exhibited pictures in more than 80 countries.
