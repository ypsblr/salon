Umberto D’Eramo

 

Born in Rome in 1954, resident and self-employed in Sulmona. Adjunct Professor in the School of Medicine, Department of Neurology at the University La Sapienza in Rome and Palermo. Scientific researcher, specializing in digitalized biometry and movement analysis. Amateur photographer since 1974. For years participant in numerous competitions with some success, as well as many shows in Italy and abroad. Winner at the X Edition of the 2015 Florence Biennale of Contemporary Art (section Digital Photography).

He has received awards from:

FIAP (Federation Internationale De L’Art Photographique) – EFIAP/s

PSA (Photographic Society of America) –MPSA
