In 2019, Aniruddha Das achieved the AFIP distinction from FIP, India. The following year, in 2020, he attained the EFIP distinction from FIP, India, and the AFIAP distinction from FIAP, France. He was honored with the HON. PESGSPC and GPA distinctions from Cyprus and Georgia, respectively, in 2020. In 2021, he earned the EFIAP distinction from FIAP, France, and the EFIP GOLD distinction from FIP, India. His accolades continued in 2022 with the EFIAP/B Distinction from FIAP, France, followed by the EFIAP/S Distinction from FIAP, France, the A.CPE Distinction from Campina, Romania, and the M.APS Distinction from APS, Bangladesh, in 2023. Since 2002, Aniruddha Das's photographs have been exhibited in various exhibition halls in Kolkata, and they have been featured in leading Indian photography magazines such as Smart Photography and Asian Photography Magazine, showcasing his photographic journey multiple times.
