David Tay is a former CEO of a leading magazine publishing group in Singapore. He retired in 2006 and continues to influence the society and the world of photography. In 2008, he became the first Asian to be elected to the Board of The International Federation of Photographic Art (FIAP). He was also appointed as Director of Promotion and Partnership in FIAP.

He is a passionate practitioner and promoter of photographic art. He serves as the President of The Photographic Society of Singapore. In recognition of his services to photographic art, he was recognized in 1982 with the Cultural Medallion award, Signapore's highest recognition in the field of art.

He had organized many international photographic events to promote photography. He has also used photography to raise funds for various causes like cancer treatment, care of elderly people and green environment. His contributions to community were recognized by his appointment as Justice of Peace by the President of Republic of Singapore in 1998. He was also bestowed with Public Service Star award in 2006.
