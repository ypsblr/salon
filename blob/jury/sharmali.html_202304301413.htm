In 2004, Sharmali Das began her journey in photography by enrolling in a course at CHHAYAPATH, CALCUTTA, a premier institution in Kolkata. After completing her Diploma in Photography in 2007, she became a member of Chhayapath, Calcutta in 2008. In 2011-12, 2014-15, 2015-16, and 2016-17, she took on the role of Treasurer for Chhayapath, Calcutta. For three consecutive years, from 2017-18 to 2019-20, she served as Salon Chairman of Chhayapath, Calcutta and oversaw the 19th and 20th Chhayapath International Salons. In 2022, Sharmali achieved EFIAP/d3, becoming the first Indian to do so. She has also earned BPSA in 2018 and GMPSA in January 2020, making her the fourth Indian and the first woman in Southeast Asia to achieve this distinction. Sharmali has traveled extensively throughout rural Bengal for her photography, which inspired her to create an organization to provide service to rural areas. She currently works as a Cost Accountant in a State-run Public Utility sector.
