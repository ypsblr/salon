Carol is an international award-winning amateur photographer from England who took up photography in 2013 after a career in Brand Marketing. 5 years later she gained a  Fellowship of The Royal Photographic Society (RPS) and achieved a raft of other photographic distinctions and qualifications. She enjoys all genres of photography but specializes in Creative Portraiture. As a member of Rolls Royce Photographic Society in the UK she was part of the team that won the FIAP World Cup this year (2023).  

Carol likes to share with others her passion for self-development which she does through mentoring, judging, lecturing and running workshops, as well as being the Judges & Lecturers Secretary of her regional photographic federation (N&EMPF).
