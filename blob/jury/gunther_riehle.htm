Gunther Riehle is a passionate Wildlife Nature photographer with the photography competition virus firmly integrated into his DNA double helix. He has been participating in photography competitions since the age of 18. He is the founder of the German Mega Circuit, the German Open Circuit, the German Southern Circuit, and My Favourite Circuit. He is an engineer for food technology / nutritional sciences by profession.

Gunther Riehle lives in Italy. For nature wildlife photography, he travels mainly to the cold of the Arctic, sub-Antarctic and Antarctic. He has contributed many articles to PSA Journal. His works have been published in leading magazines like UK The Times, Daily Mirror, The Guardian etc. He also regularly teaches photography at the local youth center and has given individual training lessons in Adobe Lightroom.

He has judged over 100 International Salons in every inhabited continent.
