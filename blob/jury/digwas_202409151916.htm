FIP Member ID : LM 013505

Digwas Bellemane has had an exemplary 10 years of experience in the space of Fine Art Photography. He leaves no stone unturned when it comes to producing unique pictures. His moonlight picture of the roaring Hogenakkal Falls is an example of the pains he takes to produce his pictures. He has won over 140 International Medals and 75 National Awards. Over 170 pictures of Digwas have earned him more than 650 Acceptances in International Salons held in 22 countries.

Born in a quaint town of Sirsi amidst the beautiful Western Ghats, in Karnataka, Digwas Bellemane’s journey in Photography took him a long way. He is a Lifetime member of the Federation of Indian Photography (FIP) and the Youth Photographic Society Bangalore(YPS) and Sagara Photographic Society. He is In-charge of FIP Distinctions Committee and FIP Salon Saathi. He is also lead of YPS journal Dṛṣṭi’s Editorial Team. In due recognition of his service, both the YPS and the FIP have conferred on him their prestigious Service Awards.
