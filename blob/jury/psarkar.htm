Mr. Parthasarathy Sarkar has a long photographic journey extending over 40 years.Banker by profession, his keen interest in photography pushed him to obtain Diploma in Photography and also complete Judges Training provided by Photographic Association of Dumdum.

He has more than 1000 acceptances to his credit from National and International Salons. He has also won many awards and medals. A proud moment in his photographic career is when he received the Best Author in China in the year 2015.

Mr. Sarkar has over 20 years of being a member of jury of many National and International Salons.
