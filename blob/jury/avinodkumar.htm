Vinod Kumar Peta's journey into the captivating realm of photography commenced amidst the era of negative film, shaping his artistic vision and honing his craft. Hailing from Hyderabad, India, Vinod has carved a distinguished path as a versatile artist, with a rich background as a photojournalist spanning nearly ten years across various esteemed regional media platforms.

He joyfully delves into diverse subjects and styles, from landscapes to nature and travel, driven by an unwavering passion for his craft.

Throughout his journey, Vinod has garnered several acknowledgments, including international distinctions such as EFIAP/B, EFIP, AIIPC, ASAP.

However, photography for him is not just a profession but a passionate endeavor, a relentless pursuit to capture the beauty and essence of the world.
