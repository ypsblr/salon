Nan Carder is a retired Registered Nurse (RN). She joined the Photographic Society of America (PSA) in 2008 and earned the following distinctions: PPSA (2011), EPSA (2013), MPSA (2015), MPSA2 (2019), GMPSA (2020). She was elected for honors APSA (2015) and FPSA (2019).

She served as the PSA Club Representative for the Lancaster Photography Association since 2010 and received the PSA Club Representative of the Year (for large clubs) in 2011. Nan served on the Southern California Council of Camera Clubs (S4C) Board as Secretary twice and served as Competition Chair in the following areas: Interclub, Nature, Photojournalism, and PhotoTravel, since 2010.

She served on the PSA Board of Directors since from 2011 to 2021 as PSA's Chapters, Clubs and Councils Vice President and as PSA Secretary. She also served on the Executive Committee. She received the President's Award in 2016.

She served on the Photographic Alliance of America (PAA) on the Executive Committee and she now serves on the PAA as a Board of Director.
