Born in an artistic family in Amalapuram, Mr Ravindranath started photography at the age of 12. Well known for pictorial photography, monochrome works, experimental and abstract photography, Mr Ravindranath is one of the top 10 monochrome exhibitors in Indian salons between 1979 to 1995, and won innumerable awards and merit certificates at the National and International photography competitions.

He is the founder of the United Foto Artists Organization (Rajahmundry) and a Telugu daily Photojournalist (Vijayawada). At present he is serving as President for Andhra Pradesh Photography Akademi. He has also organized two international and four national salons.
