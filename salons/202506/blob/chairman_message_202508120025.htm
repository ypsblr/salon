Message from the Salon Chairman

I begin with heartfelt gratitude to the 343 authors from 39 countries who contributed an astounding 4,047 photographs. This overwhelming response fills our entire team with immense pride. Thank you for sharing such magnificent and captivating work. Our salon has earned four international recognitions from leading photography federations, giving participants the opportunity to achieve their coveted distinctions.

YPS continues to foster active participation from photography clubs and groups through Best Club and Best Participant awards, and it is truly encouraging to see both Indian and international clubs embracing this opportunity. This marks our 16th International Salon, following 43 National and 15 International salons organized in the past.

The resounding success of this event is a testament to the global participation we’ve received. I am deeply grateful to my talented, dedicated, and insightful team, whose hard work made this achievement possible.

Our in-house salon judging software, developed by the late Shri Murali Santhanam, has been further refined based on feedback from previous judges. With our open-judging process, participants can view their scores live and track their image’s progress throughout the evaluation. I sincerely thank our esteemed jury of accomplished photographers in their own right for their fairness, expertise, and integrity.

It is a great honor to serve as International Salon Chairman for the third time, having also been National Salon Chairman thrice and International Salon Secretary twice. I thank the YPS Executive Committee for their trust in my leadership.

The journey of the YPS International Digital Salon 2025 has been both a joy and a valuable learning experience. My heartfelt thanks go to our Salon partners, print partners like Technova, media partners, the YPS Executive Committee, and our unwavering Salon Committee members. I also extend special gratitude to Ms. Anitha Mysore, Salon Secretary, for her steadfast support and shared responsibility in the planning and execution of this salon.

Message from the Salon Secretary

I am pleased to present a comprehensive report on the YPS International Salon 2023. This event was the third consecutive Salon I have had the privilege of organizing, and it has been a satisfying experience for all involved.

The successful management of bringing in 12 esteemed international jury members contributed to the high caliber of our event. The diversity in the jury added an international perspective to the salon's evaluation process.

We received an overwhelming and positive response from participants, underlining the reputation and prestige of the YPS International Salon. The substantial number of submissions demonstrated the trust and enthusiasm of photographers worldwide.

Our support team, led by our dedicated Chairman, Vikas, worked tirelessly throughout the event. The Chairman's responsiveness was remarkable, addressing queries almost before they were posted, and his unwavering availability, despite his busy schedule, ensured the event's smooth functioning.

Smita, our Webmaster, effectively managed her second salon. Her meticulous approach ensured that our online presence and registration process ran smoothly. Her contribution was vital to the event's seamless digital operation. Gopinath, responsible for the Salon setup was, as always, available to mend and tweak the backend whenever needed. His technical expertise ensured the smooth functioning of the event's online platform, guaranteeing that participants and judges could navigate the process effortlessly.

The reviewers played a vital role in evaluating the 3504 pictures submitted. Their untiring work ensured that the salon maintained its high standards, adhering to our commitment to quality and impartial evaluation. The Creatives team exhibited exceptional proactivity and efficiency, working tirelessly to create an engaging and memorable experience. Their creative input was instrumental in crafting the visual identity of the salon. Our media and publicity team did an outstanding job in promoting the event. Their efforts generated significant interest among photographers and art enthusiasts, expanding our reach and ensuring that the YPS International Salon received the attention it deserved.

The international jury members were very cooperative, scored the pictures per schedule and made themselves available for jury meetings without much ado despite conflicting time zones. Their discussions and insights enriched the event, fostering a sense of international camaraderie and collaboration, not to mention the wealth of knowledge brought to the table, enhancing the judging process. The results were published as scheduled, further enhancing the credibility and transparency of the YPS International Salon 2023. The timely release of results was a testament to the meticulous planning and execution of the event.

As we look forward to the upcoming physical exhibition, scheduled in a month's time, we anticipate it will be a culmination of the creative talents and hard work put into this event. The physical exhibition promises to be an opportunity for photographers and art enthusiasts to witness the beauty and artistry of photography up close.

In conclusion, the YPS International Salon 2023 was a resounding success. I would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to the YPS Executive Committee, the Salon Committee and our esteemed Chairman for their unwavering commitment and dedication. This event would not have been possible without their motivating efforts.

Thank you for entrusting me with the role of Salon Secretary, and I look forward to future opportunities to contribute to the success of YPS International Salons.
