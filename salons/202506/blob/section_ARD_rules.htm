ARCHITECTURE PIDC (COLOR Only)

This section includes images that are visually appealing and artistic compositions featuring the aesthetic and functional aspects of architecture, including the design, form, details, materials, and spatial relationships of various structures.

A good architectural image focuses on capturing and showcasing buildings, structures, and the built environment. It often emphasizes composition, lines, shapes, patterns, light, and shadow to highlight the architectural elements and create visually striking images. It can encompass a wide range of subjects, including residential and commercial buildings, landmarks, bridges, urban landscapes, interiors, and exteriors. A strong image exhibits techniques such as Framing, perspective control, and attention to lighting conditions to capture the essence and character of a structure.
