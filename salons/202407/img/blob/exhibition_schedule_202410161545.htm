Guest of Honour


Shri M C Ramesh
Deputy Director (Admn & welfare)
Dept. of Youth Empowerment and Sports,
Government. of Karnataka
 

Mudigundam Channappa Ramesh, a graduate in Science, pursued post-graduate studies at the University of Mysore, specializing in human evolution, culture, traditions, people, linguistics, food, and societal worship, with a particular focus on tribal communities. He also holds a Post-Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management and has cleared the UGC exam. Ramesh served as a Research Associate for nearly eight years in various prestigious policy-oriented research institutes before joining the Karnataka Administrative Service in 2006 as an Assistant Director in the Department of Youth Empowerment and Sports, starting his service in Dakshina Kannada. He later served in the Chief Minister's Office (CMO) for three years and was promoted to Deputy Director. During his tenure, he participated in eight Youth Festivals across various states and oversaw the organization of the National Games. Currently, he is responsible for the administration and welfare of the Sports Department.
