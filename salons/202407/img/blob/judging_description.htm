YPS Open Remote Judging

Due to the large number of entries and varied time zones of the jury members, we will carry out judging before the dates of the webcast. The scores display will be available on YPS Facebook Page and on YPS Youtube Channel as per the schedule published on the salon website. The results for every section will be announced at the beginning of the scores display of the next section, starting from the second session onwards.

It is a great opportunity to watch and enjoy all the submitted pictures from the comfort and safety of your home wherever you are around the globe. The recording will also be available on YouTube to watch later.
