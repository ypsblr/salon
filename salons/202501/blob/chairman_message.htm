Message from the Salon Chairperson

It is a privilege to stand before you as the Chairperson of the YPS National Salon 2025. I am deeply grateful to the Executive Committee for placing their trust in me and for the unwavering support that has made this journey so rewarding.

A special acknowledgment goes to Kishan Harwalkar, our Salon Secretary, whose dedication and precision have been the backbone of our operations. His tireless efforts ensured that every aspect of the Salon functioned smoothly. Likewise, the steadfast support of Vikas, the YPS President, has been invaluable. His leadership and commitment have provided strength and direction, reinforcing the foundation of our Salon.

This year, we received 2,409 works from 190 authors, a testament to the faith photographers continue to place in YPS. The creativity and passion showcased through these submissions reaffirm our mission to provide a platform that celebrates and nurtures photographic excellence.

The success of this Salon would not have been possible without our dedicated Salon committee. Their hard work behind the scenes ensured the seamless execution of every detail. Additionally, I extend my deepest gratitude to our esteemed panel of eight jury members, whose expertise and integrity upheld the highest standards in evaluating submissions.

We are also immensely grateful to our sponsors, whose generous support has played a significant role in making this Salon a success. Their contributions have enabled us to continue our mission of celebrating photographic excellence and fostering a vibrant community of artists.

As we move forward, I am excited for the future. With the continued support of our participants, team, and jury, we remain committed to fostering artistic excellence and pushing the boundaries of creativity.

Thank you all for being a part of this journey.

Message from the Salon Secretary

I am pleased to present a comprehensive report on the YPS National Salon 2025. This event was the second Salon I have had the privilege of organizing, and it has been a satisfying experience for all involved.

The successful management of bringing in 8 esteemed jury members contributed to the high caliber of our event. The diversity in the jury added a pan-india perspective to the salon's evaluation process.

We received an overwhelming and positive response from participants, underlining the reputation and prestige of the YPS National Salon. The substantial number of submissions demonstrated the trust and enthusiasm of photographers worldwide.

Our support team, led by our dedicated Chairperson, Anitha, worked tirelessly throughout the event. The Chairperson's responsiveness was remarkable, addressing queries almost before they were posted, and her unwavering availability, despite her busy schedule, ensured the event's smooth functioning.

Gopinath, our Webmaster, effectively managed the salon. His meticulous approach ensured that our online presence and registration process ran smoothly. His contribution was vital to the event's seamless digital operation. Gopinath, as always, was available to mend and tweak the backend whenever needed. His technical expertise ensured the smooth functioning of the event's online platform, guaranteeing that participants and judges could navigate the process effortlessly.

The reviewers played a vital role in evaluating the 2409 pictures submitted. Their untiring work ensured that the salon maintained its high standards, adhering to our commitment to quality and impartial evaluation. The Creatives team exhibited exceptional proactivity and efficiency, working tirelessly to create an engaging and memorable experience. Their creative input was instrumental in crafting the visual identity of the salon. Our media and publicity team did an outstanding job in promoting the event. Their efforts generated significant interest among photographers and art enthusiasts, expanding our reach and ensuring that the YPS National Salon 2025 received the attention it deserved.

The jury members were very cooperative, scored the pictures per schedule and made themselves available for jury meetings at short notice. Their discussions and insights enriched the event, fostering a sense of camaraderie and collaboration, not to mention the wealth of knowledge brought to the table, enhancing the judging process. The results are being published ahead of time, further enhancing the credibility and transparency of the YPS National Salon 2025. The timely release of results was a testament to the meticulous planning and execution of the event.

As we look forward to the upcoming physical exhibition, scheduled in May, we anticipate it will be a culmination of the creative talents and hard work put into this event. The physical exhibition promises to be an opportunity for photographers and art enthusiasts to witness the beauty and artistry of photography up close.

In conclusion, the YPS National Salon 2025 was a resounding success. I would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to the YPS Executive Committee, the Salon Committee and our esteemed Chairperson for their unwavering commitment and dedication. This event would not have been possible without their motivating efforts.

Thank you for entrusting me with the role of Salon Secretary, and I look forward to future opportunities to contribute to the success of YPS National Salons.
