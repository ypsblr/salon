AWARD FUNCTION AND SALON EXHIBITION

Salon Exhibition

The Salon Exhibition of [salon-name] was held on Saturday, October 19, 2024, and Sunday, October 20, 2024, at [exhibition-venue]. All Medal and Ribbon-winning images were printed using the high-resolution images uploaded by the participants, framed and displayed at the venue on both days.

A slideshow of all accepted images was also continuously running on a large TV at the venue.

A large number of visitors attended the exhibition.

Award Function & Catalog Release

The Award Function to distribute medals and awards was held on October 20, 2024, at 11:00 AM. Shri. Raghuram Krishnan, CFO and Senior Director of Finance at Adobe India, chaired the function. Shri. M. C. Ramesh, Deputy Director of DYES, was the guest of honor. In addition to handing out awards to the participants from Bangalore, the Chief Guest and Guest of Honour also released the Salon Catalog.

YPS Thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers!
