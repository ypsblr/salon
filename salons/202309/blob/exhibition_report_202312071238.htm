AWARD FUNCTION AND SALON EXHIBITION

Salon Exhibition

The Salon Exhibition of  [salon-name] was held from Saturday, December 02, 2023 to Sunday, December 03, 2023 at  [exhibition-venue]. All Medal and Ribbon winning images were printed using the high resolution images uploaded by the participants, framed and displayed at the venue on both days.

A slideshow of all accepted images was also continuously running on a large TV at the venue.

YPS also held a talk and slideshow on December 02, 2023 by Ms. Barbara Schmidt, also the Chief Guest for the day. She presented awe-inspiring images from her architecture collection and this was a sight to behold.

The exhibition was participated by a large number of visitors.

Award Function & Catalog Release

The Award Function to distribute medals and awards was held on December 02, 2023 at 05:00 PM. The Award function was chaired by Ms. Barbara Schmidt, a very well-known international photographer, who hails from Germany. The Guest of Honor was Shri. N Shashi Kumar (IPS), Commissioner, DYES. Apart from handing out awards for the participants from Bangalore, the Chief Guest also released the Salon Catalog. 

YPS thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers !
