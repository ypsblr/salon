It’s a proud moment for Photo artists across the world for having participated in the International Digital Salon 2021, commemorating our Golden Jubilee. Youth Photographic Society, India’s leading photography club has organized over 50 National and International salons to date and has set precedence for other salons to follow in all aspects. The quality and transparency that we at YPS strive for, cannot be matched. The Executive Committee and Salon Committee members have worked very hard to see that we got a record number of entries. Judging 17451 pictures was no mean feat. The master software has done the job perfectly in all aspects, to the utmost satisfaction of all international judges. YPS is the pioneer in open judging, allowing all the participants to witness the live display of scores, and this time, it was webcast live across the world and all participants could see their marks, in the live sessions.

The Golden Jubilee year of Youth Photographic Society is being celebrated in many ways which include workshops, exhibitions, talks etc. On the salon front, we have made both All India and International Salons free for participation. I must thank Vikas Sastry, YPS and International Salon Secretary, for suggesting this excellent idea. We have gained faith and trust from salon entrants. We consider this as an exponential service to the Art of Photography

The prestigious jury with an outstanding caliber has picked the best pictures from thousands, befitting an international salon. We are very happy with their judging and thank them for giving their precious time and patience in judging more than 4000 images in each section.

I thank all our salon participants for their overwhelming response and look forward to their continued participation in our future salons in support of the Art of Photography.

The International Photography Federations like PSA, FIAP, ICS, GPU, FIP and MoL have been kind enough to give us patronages. We are ever grateful to the office bearers of ICS, GPU, Mol and FIP for giving us the patronage, medals and ribbons free of cost, thereby making our golden jubilee memorable. I take this opportunity to thank Mr Mohammed Arfan Asif, Mr Subrata Bysak and Mr Bhupesh Little, for their love and commitment to YPS and for contributing to the success of this salon.

The unconditional support from all the committee members is laudable; if not for their support, this would not have been so successful. I dedicate this salon to both Executive and Salon Committee members.
