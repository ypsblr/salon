AWARD FUNCTION AND SALON EXHIBITION

Salon Exhibition

After many hits and misses caused by Corona measures taken by the Government, the Salon Committee and the YPS Executive Committee was finally able to put up the winning frames at the Deveraj Urs Gallery at Chitrakala Parishath, the most popular art destination in Bengaluru, India.

We received printable files for 159 of the 163 award winning images. These were printed on non-tear exhibition paper from Technova and framed to present them in their full grandeur. Each picture was accompanied by a title card showing the picture title, country flag and the author information. The YPS Salon Committee and volunteers of YPS were present to guide the visitors.

All the accepted images were displayed as a slideshow on a large TV. Each slide contained the picture title, author details, award details and, where available, EXIF information. Many visitors sat in front of the display and enjoyed the slideshow.

The exhibition was attended by around 200 visitors.

Award Function & Catalog Release

The Award Function was held on Sunday, February 27 from 11:00 AM to 01:00 PM. The Chief Guest for the Award function was Dr. M Jagadeesh, Joint Director of Horticulture, Department of Horticulture, Government of Karnataka. Dr. Jagadeesh is a nature lover and manages Lalbagh, one of the largest lung spaces of Bangalore.

Dr. Jagadeesh, during his speech praised the people who take photographs, the audience who have come to view the photographs, without whom the photographs have no meaning, and volunteers of Youth photographic Society, who made it possible for the photographs to reach the audience.

The Catalog was released by the Chief Guest, Dr. Jagadeesh, the YPS President & Salon Chairman, Satish Hanumantharao and the YPS Secretary & Salon Secretary, Vikas Sastry. The Chief Guest also presented the awards to a few paticipants who could join in person. Due to uncertainities around the exhibition, all the awards had already been shipped to the recipients prior to the Exhibition.

During the event, YPS also recognized Mr. Satheesh B Seshadri and the Technova team for their continuous support to YPS Salons and events. Technova India also celebrates 50 years of service in India.

YPS Thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers !
