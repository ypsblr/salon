YPS REMOTE JUDGING

A Warm invitation to join Judging Session

The YPS International Salon 2021 Salon Committee sincerely thanks all the participants, supporters and volunteers for the overwhelming support for the Salon. The YPS Salon Committee wishes the participants all success. Considering the large number of pictures, remote scoring will start ahead of time and will continue till the date of judging. Scores display for all the pictures will start during the afternoon on the date of judging. Announcement of winners for the section will be made duribg the event on the following day.

YPS Open Remote Judging

Due to the large number of entries, we will carry out judging over 4 days, one section on each day. The scores display will be available on YPS Facebook Live and on YPS Youtube Live from 4:00 PM India time each day. The results for the section will be announced the same day at the end of scores display.

It is a great opportunity to watch and enjoy all the submitted pictures from the comfort and safety of your home wherever you are around the globe. Recording will also be available on Youtube to watch later.

Please check the schedule below and join us in witnessing the scores.
