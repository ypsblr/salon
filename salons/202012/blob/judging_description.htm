YPS OPEN JUDGING

A Warm invitation to join Judging

We thank all those who participated in the Salon. The YPS Salon Committee wishes them all success.

In 2020 YPS shifted to Remote Judging on account of restrictions imposed by the pandemic and to take advantage of opportunity to bring-in jury from different locations. However, YPS continues to uphold the tradition of Open Judging, where the participants and public have the opportunity to witness live scores. A new system was developed to meet this challenge and the same was used in the YPS All India Salon 2020 with resounding success. Now we are extending this to Global Open Judging with jury members separated by oceans and timezones. Be a witness to this grand event.

Watching Open Judging

Live scores along with pictures will be webcast through the YPS Accounts on Facebook Live and Youtube Live. Click on the links below to our Facebook and Thumbnail pages and Follow / Subscribe to be notified of the event.
