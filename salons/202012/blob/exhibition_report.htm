YPS SALON EXHIBITION REPORT

3 Days of exhibition ending on World Photography Day

YPS International Salon 2019 ended in grand style with an exhibition of award winning and accepted images and presentation of Awards on the last day of the exhibition. All the digital images winning Medals and Ribbons were printed on Epson printer by Prism Giclee Prints on special matte paper provided by Technova and were displayed in nice frames. This made the award winning images look even better and attracted wide appreciation. All the Accepted images were displayed on 42 inch TV in a running slideshow that repeated every hour. The Salon Exibition was visited by over 200 people in the three days. Most of the visitors enjoyed the lovely pictures from all over the world. Some wanted the pictures to be displayed in larger format.

The Award Ceremony was held on the morning on the last date, to coincide with World Photography Day. The function was presided over by Mr. Nagesh Hegde, Noted Journalist and Columnist and by Mr. Harish Sejekan, Renowned Curator, Artist and Actor. The Salon Catalog was released during the function. The winners from Karnataka who could attend in person were presented with Medals/Ribbons and Certificates. The Certificates were printed by Technova in Non-Tear sheets.

YPS also celebrated World Photography on the same day with many recognitions to noted photographers and service awards for YPS volunteers.

Shri. T L Ramaswamy, Retired Photographer, Deccan Herald was honored with YPS Lifetime Achievement Award for his contributions to Photo Journalism. His works were widely appreciated by photographers, journalists and noted personalities. Mr. Satish, while speaking about Mr. T L Ramaswamy mentioned that not a single issue of the daily newspaper was released without his photograph.

Shri. Seturam Neginhal, Retired Officer, Indian Forest Services was presented with Honorary YPS in appreciation of his dedicated services as a Forester, including as Urban Forestry Preservator, Wildlife Conservator, Author & Discoverer and as an accomplished Wildlife Photographer.

Shri. M VIshwanath, Founder Member of YPS, was presented with Honorary YPS in appreciation of his dedicated services as a writer, artist and photographer.

Shri. Digwas Bellemane, Life Member of YPS, was presented with YPS Service Award in appreciation of his dedicated services to the YPS Salon Group that has grown to over 30 members and had helped YPS win more than 10 Best Club Awards in less than 6 months.

Shri. Manju Vikas Sastry V, Secretary, YPS, was presented with YPS Service Award in appreciation of his dedicated services to YPS in bringing about significant reforms and in functioning effectively as a single point of contact to all the members.

The Salon Exhibition closed on a high note with excellent feedback from visitors, Salon participants and YPS Members and with wide media coverage with news shared in 5+ magazines and 2 TV channels.

All participants were provided with links to view/download Salon Catalog and also to download certificates in PDF Format. It is time to completed sending of medals and certificates and take the Salon to a closure.

YPS Thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers !
