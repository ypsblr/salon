SALON RESULTS - CONGRATULATIONS

Salon Results are now ready, ahead of time, as on December 1st. Individual results were mailed to each participant with scores for each picture along with links to upload printable files for exhibition printing. In addition, scores are also available on My Page.

International Flavor

This International Salon brought true international flavor to the Salon in many ways. We received submissions from 131 authors from 50 international countries bringing in a variety of subjects and approaches. With 8 out of 12 juries coming from other countries, we experienced, for the first time, the divergent perspectives on photography from across the globe.

In summary, YPS International Salon 2020 is nothing like any of the Salons YPS handled in the past.

Internationalisation of Judging

YPS has successfully handled dealing with 5 different timezones and holiday schedules and completed the judging in just 2 days. We also had to make improvements to our judging software to make it user friendly so that the jury can work without our constant support.
