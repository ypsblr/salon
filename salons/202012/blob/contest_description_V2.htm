About the Salon

Salon Theme

YPS International Salon 2019 is a DIGITAL and PRINT Salon to facilitate participants to be able to qualify for some of the international honors. The Salon enjoys patronage from popular international Photographic Associations.

A Very Special Salon

YPS International Salon 2019 is now open for participation with some exciting new features:

  • It is a DIGITAL and PRINT Salon. Win acceptances under PRINT sections to be able to qualify for those honors that have been eluding so far.
  • Popular Digital Sections: MONOCHROME OPEN DIGITAL, COLOR OPEN DIGITAL, NATURE DIGITAL, TRAVEL DIGITAL.
  • Popular Print Sections: MONOCHROME OPEN PRINT, COLOR OPEN PRINT.
  • Patronages from FIAP, PSA, GPU, MOL and FIP. There are 105 medals and pins and badges to be won.
  • Printing partners to make it convenient to participate in Print sections. If you want to submit prints, you can use the services of these printers to get them printed in India and mailed to us. You also save on postages and delays in submission.
  • Highly subsidized fees to reduce your financial burden.
  • Further concessions for Clubs and Groups. Participants can create the club/group online and add members very easily.
  • All payments are through fully integrated on-line gateways with Paypal support for international participants. No need to wait for payment to be verified.
  • Past participants can save time by logging in using pre-created credentials. No need to register again.
  • Professionally managed with quick responses to all queries raised through the Salon website.

Get started today. You can always substitute your pictures or even change sections till the last moment.
