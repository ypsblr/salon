About YPS International Salon 2017

YPS International Digital Salon 2017 is our first All-Digital International Salon with on-line payment options. Conducted after a gap of nearly 10 years, we were overwhelmed by the response and we thank all the participants. Youth Photographic Society thanks all the participants for participating in the Salon and all the judges for taking time to pick up these awards. It was a tough competition and we congratulate all the winners for emerging victorious. The winning images have been selected from 2821 pictures uploaded by 281 participants from 24 countries.

Youth Photographic Society Bengaluru (YPS) is pleased to announce the results for the YPS International Digital Salon 2017 under recognitions from FIAP, PSA and FIP. The Salon was closed for participation at the midnght of July 27th, GMT time. The judging of the Salon pictures was held on July 29th & 30th in an Open Judging Event organized by YPS at JP Nagar Cultural Association, Bengaluru, India.

The International Salon held after a gap of nearly 12 years had many firsts for YPS.

  • This is the first International "Digital" Salon for YPS.
  • This is the first time YPS accepted pictures in 1920x1200 pixel high resolution for judging. Projected in 100% resolution on an Epson Full HD projector, the juries could see all the details required to choose the best ones.
  • This is the first time YPS used its home-grown Judging software on an International Salon to make a fair and quick selection of the finalists. Continuing the tradition of YPS, the judging was open to observers and nearly 50 persons participated. The observers could see live display of images with scores as they are being scored by the judges. They could listen the conversation between judges during finalization of pictures.
  • This is the first time, the entrants could pay on-line using multiple payment options in Indian Rupees as well as in US Dollars.

The results were published on the day after the judging on July 31st as against the planned date of August 8th. YPS is pleased with the outcome of the Salon. Here are some statistics to relish.

  • Number Entrants who have uploaded pictures is 281. We also had 24 women photographers among them.
  • Number of countries participating is 23. 20% of the Entrants were from outside India. The largest overseas participation was from China.
  • Number of pictures uploaded is 2821.
  • Young and Experienced entrants participated in the Salon with age ranging from 17 to 84. The average age of participants in 46. The maximum number of Entrants were in the age range of 31 to 40 years. Four entrants were less than 20 years in age. YPS is encouraged by participation at a young age. There were 2 entrants older than 80 years. YPS appreciates the undying spirit of participation.
  • 72 Awards and 621 acceptances were provided for pictures covering 24.5% of the overall pictures submitted. After India, the highest number of wins went to China followed by Hong Kong, Russia, UK, Singapore, USA and Taiwan. Every one of the 23 countries that participated had won either an Award or Acceptance. 203 of the 281 entrants either won an Award or Acceptance. 8 Entrants had 10+ Awards/Acceptances. 30 entrants had between 5 and 9 Awards/Acceptances. The Women Photographers walked away with 9 awards and 78 acceptances.
  • The FIAP Blue Pin for the Best Entrant went to Mr. Arnaldo Paulo Che from Hong Kong S.A.R. The fact that of the 16 pictures submitted, 8 won Awards and 4 more won Acceptances, puts Mr. Arnaldo Paulo Che way ahead of others. YPS Congratulates Mr. Arnaldo Paulo Che for this remarkable participation. His award details can be found under OVERALL AWARDS on the Results Page.
  • The Best Participating Club is Shutter Bugs Creative Forum from Dubai. With 14 entrants, 3 Awards and 59 acceptances, the Club stands tall among others. YPS thanks Shutter Bugs Creative Forum for participating in the Salon and congratulates all the members.
