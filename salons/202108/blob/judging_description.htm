YPS OPEN REMOTE JUDGING

A Warm invitation to join Judging

The YPS All India Digital Salon 2021 Salon Committee sincerely thanks all the participants, supporters and volunteers for the overwhelming support for the Salon. The YPS Salon Committee wishes the participants all success. YPS will continue with our established practice of YPS OPEN REMOTE JUDGING for this salon too. YPS has been known for the open judging process in which the scoring is made public in the Judging Hall through live display and beyond through webcast.

New YPS Open Remote Judging

Due to the large number of entries, we will carry out judging over 4 days, one section on each day. The live scores will be available on YPS Facebook Page and on YPS Youtube Page from 9:30 AM each day. The results for the section will announced during the start of next day's program.

Watch your fortunes unfold live, in the comfort and safety of your home.

Please check the schedule below and join us in witnessing the scores as the Jury members score the pictures from all over India.
