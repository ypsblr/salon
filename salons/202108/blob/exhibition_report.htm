AWARD FUNCTION AND INAUGURATION OF EXHIBITION

Salon Exhibition

Due to the relaxations made by the Govt. of Karnataka, the Salon Committee and the YPS Exceutive Committee decided to hold a print exhibition for three days from Friday, August 20 to Sunday, August 22 at Chitrakala Parishath, the hub of art, in Bangalore. Of course, all precautions were taken to see that the exhibition and function conforms to the covid norms.

We received printable files for 67 of the 69 award winning images. These were printed on non-tear exhibition paper from Technova and framed to present them in full grandeur. Each picture was accompanied by title cards showing the picture title and the author information. The YPS Salon Committee and volunteers of YPS were present to guide the visitors.

All the accepted images were displayed as a slideshow on a large TV. Each slide contained the picture title, author details, award details and, where available, EXIF information. Many visitors sat in front of the display and enjoyed the slideshow.

Thanks to the efforts made by K S Rajaram, Salon Committee Member, the exhibition gained wide publicity on many newspapers like Prajavani and Deccan Herald. Doordarshan covered the function and telecast in on their news channel on August 21st. Click to see the news clip.

The exhibition was attended by around 250 visitors.

Award Function

The Award Function was held on Saturday, August 21 from 10:30 AM to 1:00 PM. The Chief Guest for the Award function was Dr. Shivakumar Begar, a veteran Yakshagana Artist and art lover. The Guest of Honor, Shri. Venkatesh Kumar, IAS, Commissioner, Department of Youth Empowerment and Sports could not attend due to last minute need to travel. He sent his best wishes for the success of the function.

Dr. Shivakumar, during his speech praised the people who take photographs, the audience who have come to view the photographs, without whom the photographs have no meaning, and volunteers of Youth photographic Society, who made it possible for the photographs to reach the audience. He also mentioned that the photographers have a third eye to identify the scenes that made an impact and to present them in a powerful way.

The Catalog was released by the Chief Guest, Dr. Shivakumar Begar, the YPS President, Satish Hanumantharao, the Salon Chair Person, Prema Kakade and the Salon Secretary, Krishna Bhat. The Chief Guest also presented the awards to paticipants who could join in person. We thank all those who have tyravelled fraom afar to join us during the Award Ceremony.

During the event, YPS also recognized members of YPS who achieved International Distinctions and also presented Service Awards for the years 2019 and 2020, recognizing voulnteers of YPS who made significant contribuitions to the success of YPS.

YPS Thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers !
