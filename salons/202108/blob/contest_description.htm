The Golden Jubilee Salon

YPS is happy to announce our most prestigious All India Salon during the year of Golden Jubilee. YPS started with a handful of enthusiasts in 1971 and has blossomed into one of the oldest and largest photography clubs in India, in the World. YPS, continuing its journey to promote photography, has bundled several exciting features into this salon:

  • It is absolutely FREE for all participants.
  • There are two exciting YPS Golden Jubilee Awards under each section in honor of Legends of Photography from Karnataka. This is something every photographer should covet to receive.
  • Acceptance is reported for Youth participants as well.
  • There are Cash Awards associated with each medal and ribbon won in the Salon.
  • Participants will continue to enjoy all the other features of YPS Salons, including 100% review of pictures, Live Judging, Virtual Exhibition and Certificates for all Acceptances.

[contest-name] is open for participation from [registration-start-date] to photographers from all over India.
