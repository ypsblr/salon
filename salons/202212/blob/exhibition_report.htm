AWARD FUNCTION AND SALON EXHIBITION

Salon Exhibition

The Salon Exhibition of  [salon-name] was held from Friday, January 20, 2023 to Sunday, January 22, 2023 at  [exhibition-venue]. All Medal and Ribbon winning images were printed using the high resolution images uploaded by the participants, framed and displayed at the venue on all the three days.

A slideshow of all accepted images was also continuously running on a large TV at the venue.

YPS also held a talk and slideshow on January 21, 2023 by the H Satish, President of YPS on Ladakh, its landscape and culture. 

The exhibition was participated by a large number of visitors.

Award Function & Catalog Release

The Award Function to distribute medals and awards was held on January 22, 2023 at 11:00 AM. The Award function was chaired by Mr. Krishna Setty C S, former Administrator and Chairman of Lalit Kala Academy, New Delhi. The Guest of Honor was the popular folk fusion music singer, Mr. Vasu Dixit. Apart from handing out awards for the participants from Bangalore, the Chief Guest and the Guest of Honor also released the Salon Catalog. YPS also recognized volunteers of YPS with Service Awards and also recognized members of YPS who have obtained international distinctions during the last year.

YPS Thanks all the participants, Salon Committee Members, YPS Executive Committee Members and Volunteers !
