About 34th YPS All India Digital Salon Of Photography 2016

The 34th YPS All India Digital Salon of Photography 2016 is dedicated to Late N S Ranganathan, FIRST Life Member of YPS.

Youth Photographic Society is sponsored By The Directorate Of Youth Services, Karnataka, and is a Corporate Member: Federation Of Indian Photography. This YPS Salon Has 5 Digital Sections.

The exhibition is open to all photographers, amateurs and professionals, worldwide, except members of the jury. An entry may be rejected when the sponsoring organization or its agents, in its reasonable discretion, believes the entry does not conform to the exhibition rules and conditions!
