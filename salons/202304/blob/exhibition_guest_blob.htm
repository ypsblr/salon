Ms. Deep Contractor loves nature and has always been fascinated by natural phenomena, especially the world of animals and their interaction with their environment and she chose to make a career out of it. Her passion for nature and wildlife first led her to pursue an MSc in Wildlife Science followed by her selection to the Indian Forest Service (IFS). She completed PG Diploma in Environmental Law during the pandemic. She is committed to wildlife conservation and environment protection and always looks for opportunities to acquire new knowledge. 

Overcoming challenges and learning from them is second nature to her. Her mother being her inspiration for this. Her mother single-handedly raised her and her sister while acquiring new skills throughout her career and continues to do so after retirement. She wholeheartedly supported Ms Contractor for the 'unconventional' education and career choices and so wishes to follow in her mother's footsteps and never be afraid to try new things or to ever stop learning.

As an IFS officer, she primarily implemented the policies of the Ministry of Environment, Forests and Climate Change (MoEFCC), Government of India.  She carries out activities such as forest protection and wildlife conservation that directly contribute in combating climate change. She actively works towards mitigation of human -wildlife conflict. She is also involved in implementing outreach programs for generating awareness and support for the cause of environment and forests especially among the most vulnerable communities.

Other achievements:
She is a licensed open water scuba diver and is working towards getting her advanced diving certification as well. 

She is the first woman IFS Officer to participate in an expedition to Antarctica and completed the expedition in March 2022. 

She is currently working as Director of Biligiri Ranganatha Swami Temple (BRT) Tiger Reserve. She is arguably the first and as of now the only woman Tiger Reserve Director serving in the state of Karnataka.
