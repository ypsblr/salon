A Warm invitation to join Judging Session

The  [salon-name]  Salon Committee sincerely thanks all the participants, supporters and volunteers for the overwhelming support for the Salon. The YPS Salon Committee wishes the participants all success. Considering the large number of pictures, remote scoring will start ahead of time and will continue till the date of judging. We will carry out judging over 2 days, two sections on each day. Scores display for all the pictures will start in the morning on the date of judging. 

YPS Open Remote Judging

The scores display will be available on YPS Facebook Live and on YPS Youtube Live from 10:00 AM India time each day. The results for the section will be announced the same day at the end of scores display.

It is a great opportunity to watch and enjoy all the submitted pictures from the comfort and safety of your home wherever you are around the globe. Recording will also be available on Youtube to watch later.
