About the Salon

Welcome to [contest-name] !

Youth Photographic Society (YPS) is happy to welcome you to participate in [contest-name]. YPS started functioning in 1971. Growing from strength to strength, YPS blossomed into one of the largest clubs in India. Over the years, YPS has firmly established itself in the world of Salons as one of the most professionally run Salons. We aim to provide you with a rich participating experience. YPS, continuing its journey to promote photography, has bundled several exciting features into this salon:

  • Specific messages
  • Participants will continue to enjoy all the other features of YPS Salons:
    • 100% review of submitted pictures and notification to a participant for correction
    • Facility to revise pictures till the last date
    • Live Scores display during judging
    • Digital Certificates for all Acceptances
    • Auto-generated image for sharing your success on social media
    • Professionally designed Catalog with an option to order a printed catalog
    • Physical Exhibition and Slideshow
