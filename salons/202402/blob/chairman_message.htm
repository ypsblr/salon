Message from the Salon Chairperson

It is with great honor and humility that I address you for the first time as the Chairperson of the YPS National Salon 2024. I extend my heartfelt gratitude to the Executive Committee for entrusting me with this significant responsibility. Your confidence in my leadership is deeply appreciated, and I am committed to upholding the high standards and traditions of our prestigious salon.

I am fortunate to have found invaluable support in Kishan Harwalkar, the Salon Secretary, whose relentless dedication and tireless efforts have been instrumental in keeping our operations seamless and efficient. 

Behind the scenes, Vikas, the YPS President, has consistently provided steadfast support, always ready to step up whenever needed. His powerful presence has been a source of strength and reliability, ensuring that every aspect of our Salon runs smoothly. Working alongside such dedicated individuals has been a pleasure and an inspiration.

This year, we were delighted to receive an overwhelming 2753 works from 206 authors. This remarkable participation is a testament to the trust and confidence that our participants have in us. It is their unwavering enthusiasm and continuous support that motivate us to overcome the challenges we face from time to time. Your engagement and creativity are the lifeblood of our Salon, and we are immensely grateful for your contributions.

I would also like to extend my sincere thanks to the Salon committee, whose unwavering support has been pivotal in our journey. Their dedication and hard work have ensured that we maintain the high standards that our Salon is known for. Additionally, our team of 12 jury members deserves special mention. Their cooperation, professionalism, and excellent judgment have been crucial in evaluating the submissions and upholding the integrity of our salon.

As we look forward to the future, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. Together, with the continued support of our participants, committee members, and jury, I am confident that we will achieve even greater heights. Let us continue to foster creativity, innovation, and excellence in everything we do.

Thank you once again for your trust and support.

Message from the Salon Secretary

I am pleased to present a comprehensive report on the YPS National Salon 2024. This event was the first Salon I have had the privilege of organizing, and it has been a satisfying experience for all involved.

The successful management of bringing in 12 esteemed jury members contributed to the high caliber of our event. The diversity in the jury added a pan-india perspective to the salon's evaluation process.

We received an overwhelming and positive response from participants, underlining the reputation and prestige of the YPS National Salon. The substantial number of submissions demonstrated the trust and enthusiasm of photographers worldwide.

Our support team, led by our dedicated Chairperson, Anitha, worked tirelessly throughout the event. The Chairperson's responsiveness was remarkable, addressing queries almost before they were posted, and her unwavering availability, despite her busy schedule, ensured the event's smooth functioning.

Smita, our Webmaster, effectively managed her third salon. Her meticulous approach ensured that our online presence and registration process ran smoothly. Her contribution was vital to the event's seamless digital operation. Gopinath, responsible for the Salon setup was, as always, available to mend and tweak the backend whenever needed. His technical expertise ensured the smooth functioning of the event's online platform, guaranteeing that participants and judges could navigate the process effortlessly.

The reviewers played a vital role in evaluating the 2753 pictures submitted. Their untiring work ensured that the salon maintained its high standards, adhering to our commitment to quality and impartial evaluation. The Creatives team exhibited exceptional proactivity and efficiency, working tirelessly to create an engaging and memorable experience. Their creative input was instrumental in crafting the visual identity of the salon. Our media and publicity team did an outstanding job in promoting the event. Their efforts generated significant interest among photographers and art enthusiasts, expanding our reach and ensuring that the YPS National Salon 2024 received the attention it deserved.

The jury members were very cooperative, scored the pictures per schedule and made themselves available for jury meetings at short notice. Their discussions and insights enriched the event, fostering a sense of camaraderie and collaboration, not to mention the wealth of knowledge brought to the table, enhancing the judging process. The results are being published ahead of time, further enhancing the credibility and transparency of the YPS National Salon 2024. The timely release of results was a testament to the meticulous planning and execution of the event.

As we look forward to the upcoming physical exhibition, scheduled in a month's time, we anticipate it will be a culmination of the creative talents and hard work put into this event. The physical exhibition promises to be an opportunity for photographers and art enthusiasts to witness the beauty and artistry of photography up close.

In conclusion, the YPS National Salon 2024 was a resounding success. I would like to extend my heartfelt gratitude to the YPS Executive Committee, the Salon Committee and our esteemed Chairperson for their unwavering commitment and dedication. This event would not have been possible without their motivating efforts.

Thank you for entrusting me with the role of Salon Secretary, and I look forward to future opportunities to contribute to the success of YPS National Salons.
