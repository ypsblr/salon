WELCOME TO SALON EXHIBITION

A Warm invitation to visit Salon Exhibition

Year 2020 has been a year of "No Contact". All the participants submitted their pictures from the comfort of their homes. The members of jury scored and selected winning images from the safety of their abodes. And then, the Salon Committee debated whether to wait for the no-contact-period to end or to host the Award Ceremony as originally planned. Being conscious of completing things as planned, the YPS Salon Committee decided in favour of a Remote Award Ceremony and Salon Exhibition. We will, sadly, be missing the pleasure of seeing such wonderful images in print and frame and the pleasure of meeting the authors this year.

There is always a blessing in disguise behind every disappointment. Now the Exhibition can be visited by all participants and their families and friends, wherever they are, without compromising their health. Also the Exhibition will be kept open for a longer time. So let's enjoy the positivity behind the Virtual world.

The [contest-name] Virtual Exhibition will be open to everyone after the Award Ceremony on [exhibition-start-date] and will remain open for over a month till [exhibition-end-date].

And, there will be no restrictions on Visiting Hours for the Exhibition !
