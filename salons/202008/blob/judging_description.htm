YPS OPEN REMOTE JUDGING

A Warm invitation to join Judging

The YPS All India Digital Salon 2020 Salon Committee sincerely thanks all the participants, supporters and volunteers for the overwhelming support for the Salon. The YPS Salon Committee wishes the participants all success. YPS is opening another chapter this years by launching YPS OPEN REMOTE JUDGING. YPS has been known for the open judging process in which the scoring is made public in the Judging Hall through live display and beyond through webcast.

New YPS Open Remote Judging

YPS does not want to lose this tradition, just because we have to remain at our homes forced by the Corona situation. Hence we have come up with a judging process in which judging of each section will be taken up at specific time slots and the live scores will be made available through YPS Facebook page as a Facebook Live webcast. At the specified time, the Jury will be enabled to start scoring each picture and the latest complete score of each picture will be telecast live through Facebook Live. As in the past, the scoring will be in random order. Post scoring the jury will select award winning pictures through a consensus process. Due to technical limitations the award selection process is not planned to be made live.

Watch your fortunes unfold live, in the comfort and safety of your home. Here is how you can watch YPS Open Judging:

  1. Follow YPS on Facebook : https://www.facebook.com/ypsbengaluru/
  2. Open YPS Facebook Live webcast at the scheduled time to watch the scores unfold : https://facebook.com/ypsbengaluru/live/
