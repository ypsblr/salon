New features on Salon Website

We have added several new features to the Salon web-site to enhance your experience of participating in the YPS Salon. Look at these new features.

  • Author Videos - Since we are missing the chance of interacting with the photographer at the exhibition, we requested the authors to share their experience of shooting the picture. Many have taken time to record short videos on each picture. These can be played from the Resuls Page. Check it.
