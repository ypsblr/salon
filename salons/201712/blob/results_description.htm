SALON Results

Youth Photographic Society Bengaluru (YPS) is pleased to announce the results for the YPS 35th All India Salon 2017 under recognitions from FIP. The Salon was closed for participation at the midnght of December 15, 2017 at 6:00 PM IST. The judging of the Salon pictures was held on December 16th & 17th in an Open Judging Event held at JP Nagar Cultural Association, Bengaluru, India. The Judging event was participated by over 50 participants.

The 35th All India Salon is an All-Digital Salon, the second All India Digital Salon for YPS. Here are some highlights of the Salon:

  • This is the first All India Salon in which YPS accepted pictures in 1920x1200 pixel high resolution for judging. Projected in 100% resolution on an Epson Full HD projector, the juries could see all the details required to choose the best ones.
  • Apart from providing Acceptances under FIP Recognition, the Salon also offered over 52 Cash + Certificate/Medal Awards totalling over Rs. 80,000 in value. All the Awards are sponsored. See the Award List for a list of Sponsors.
  • For the first time YPS has included an exclusive segment for Young Photographers below 18 years. Participation was free. There are 12 exclusive awards only for the Youth.
  • The Salon eased Group participation by allowing any member to create a group of 5 or more members to avail discounted rates.
  • YPS Members could login using their existing password.
  • The participants have the flexibility of choosing any section to participate and the choice of sections could be changed dynamically any time. They can also pay and add more sections any time during the contest.

The results were published individually mailed to all the participants on December 22nd ahead of the planned date of December 25th. Here are some statistics to relish.
