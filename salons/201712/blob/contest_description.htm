About 35th YPS All India Salon

YPS 35th All India Digital Salon 2017 is an All-Digital salon with plenty of awards to win. This year we have also opened a Youth Section to encourage budding young photographers.

The 35th All India Digital Salon 2017 brings with it the pride of participation. The Salons of YPS are keenly contested and professionally conducted. The Salon carries FIP recognition (FIP 146/2017) to help all those planning to earn recognitions. Here are some of the salient features of the YPS 35th All India Digital Salon:

  • Special Youth participation at no cost to encourage budding photographers
  • Open Judging with public participation to ensure a fully transparent judging of pictures
  • Full Resolution Screening in 1920x1200 resolution to make sure that your picture is displayed with all the details
  • Ability to change the portfolio till the last moment to put up your latest and best pictures for judging
  • Web-site designed to international standards with high availability to handle any amount of rush
  • Payment through online portal for hazzle free participation
  • Email-assistance through the web-site page
  • Legacy of completing Salons on time
