OPEN JUDGING REPORT

An Enjoyable 3 Days

As in the past YPS managed 3 days of Open Judging for this Salon too. For the first time, YPS also went live during scoring stages for all the sections. Over 50 participants from all over the globe watched the live score sitting at their desk. An equal number of photo lovers, young and experienced, witnessed the scoring as well as the award finalization proceedings at the venue. These people had the opportunity to experience how the judges pick the winning images. They also had the opportunity to interact with the judges and gain knowledge. Results were immediately posted on the live stream for the benefit of remote participants.

Judging is an involved process requiring concentration. The judges spent over 2 hours on each section to assign individual scores and spent another hour or more to finalize images. YPS took every care to make the days of judging enjoyable, right from travel, stay, food and tools available for judging process. YPS used its time tested and unique judging software to help judges identify winning images with ease.

We want to take this opportunity to thank all the members of the jury, people who joined at the venue to make the event memorable, people who joined from remote locations, YPS Executive Committee members, Salon Committee members and volunteers who helped during the judging.
