Salon Best Practices

Enhance Chances of Winning

YPS used multiple guidelines and actions to ensure that the winning chances of the participants are enhanced:

  • Pictures that do not qualify for any of the following reasons were notified to the participants ahead of the last date, provided the pictures were uploaded early:
    1. Picture content vilating the Salon Rules
    2. Pictures that were already accepted in past YPS Salons
    3. Watermarks and other personal identifications
  • Pictures posted in lower resolutions
  • Pictures posted under wrong sections
  • Duplicate submissions of the same picture under different saections were eliminated from receiving acceptances.
