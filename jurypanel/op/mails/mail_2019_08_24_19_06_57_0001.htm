YPS International Salon 2019


Dear RAKESH VC,

We would like to thank you once again for participating in the YPS International Salon 2019 hosted by Youth Photographic Society, Bangalore, India. The Salon Exhibition and Award function were held as planned at Chitrakala Parishath. The Salon exhibited in print and frame all the pictures whose full resolution files were uploaded by the participants and all the prints submitted under PRINT sections. The Salon Catalog was released by the Chief Guest during the Award function. The Chief Guest also presented the medals and ribbons to the award winners who could attend the Function in person.

Salon Catalog Released

The Salon Catalog is released as a Digital Catalog. You can View Catalog Online or Download the Catalog.

Ordering Printed Catalog

If you like to order a printed catalog, you can send an email to salon@ypsbengaluru.com. The catalog will be printed in 8x10 inch size. Here is the cost of the catalog:

Participants from India

Rs. 1800 + Mailing Charges

Participants from Other Countries

USD 27 + Mailing Charges

After placing the order, YPS will send instructions to make the payment. Catalog printing and mailing will be initiated after receipt of payment. The last date for receipt of orders for Printed Salon Catalog is September 10, 2019.

Distribution of Awards/Prizes and Certificates

We thank everyone who could attend the Inaugural Function. Here is the arrangement for distribution of Prizes/Awards and Certificates for those who could not attend:

  • Mementos/Medals/Prizes/Certificates related to Awards and Honorable Mentions will be sent through post.
  • A Digital Certificate can be downloaded for each picture Accepted either by logging into the Salon web-site or by using the links provided in the email.
